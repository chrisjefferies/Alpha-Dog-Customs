quality & professionalism

The secret is a track record of trust and excellence.

  • cleaning

    General reconditioning includes complete disassembly, carbon deposit removal and polish of moving parts. Rates vary by firearm type.

  • furniture

    Refinishing of wood stocks includes removal of mars and scuffs, dent treatment and fresh stain and lacquer to customer specification. Not recommended for collectible firearms.

  • sights & scopes

    Modifications to firearm recievers including drilling and tapping for attaching rails, scopes or other optics. Pistol sights can be adjusted or upgraded.

  • finish

    Application of a variety of protective coatings including conventional and hot-salt bluing, Duracote, Ceracote, anodizing, chrome plating and perkerization.

  • rechambering

    Modification of firearms for new calibers, or to accept modified case designs like Ackley Improved cartridges.
