Mid Michigan's Collectible Firearm Source

Used and Collectible Firearms

Stop in to browse our excellent inventory of collectible firearms. We strive to keep our shelves stocked with some of the finest guns produced by man. While our inventory is constantly changing, we often stock high grade M1 Garands, World War II era British and German rifles, and 19th century domestic rifles.

We also specialize in historic firearm repair and restoration. If you bring a collectible firearm to Alpha Dog, you can trust that it will be handled with respect and care, and that all work will be completed in-house with very few exceptions.

New Firearms

Brick-and-mortar service. Internet prices.

If you're looking to make a new firearm purchase, look no further than Alpha Dog. We can get almost any off the shelf rifle, handgun or shotgun out the door within a few days with convenient after-hours pickup times available.

Keeping a low inventory of new firearms also allows us to pass the savings on to you. Our competitively priced guns blow the competition away, and allows us to compete with online retailers rather than brick and mortar stores.

Call or stop in to speak to one of our experienced firearms experts, and we'll get you the best deal on your next hunting, defensive or recreational firearm purchase. In the rare instance that you can find a better deal somewhere else, we offer FFL transfers for a small fee, and our gunsmithing professionals are always willing to offer inspection and maintenance services on arrival.
